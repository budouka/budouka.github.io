About Me

I’m a credit risk specialist, data scientist, and business researcher with broad experience spanning risk controlling within the banking sector as well as applied economic research. With a strong passion for data, I leverage a diverse analytical toolkit to solve complex problems and deliver useful insights.

I hold a doctorate in Economics, a Master’s degree in Management & Marketing, and a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from Freie Universität Berlin. My academic background is further enriched by international study experience in Japan and China.

With over nine years of professional experience, I have led and executed a wide range of analytical projects across the full data science lifecycle—from data collection and integrating complex datasets from multiple sources, through data cleaning, analysis, and model development, to presenting insights clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders alike.

I am analytical, proactive, and committed to continuous learning and professional development. I take ownership of my work and thrive in environments that challenge me to grow and deliver results.
